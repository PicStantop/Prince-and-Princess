About Us

Prince & Princess Maternity Clinic was established in 2005 with a mission to provide exceptional maternity care in a warm, supportive environment.

Our team of experienced obstetricians, gynecologists, midwives, and nurses are dedicated to providing personalized care that respects the unique needs and preferences of each patient.

This Clinic was licensed with the Corporate Affairs Commission Nigeria in the year 2020

Facilities and Services

  • 15-Bed Nursing Home: Offers deluxe, semi-deluxe, and general rooms for patient comfort.
  • Painless Delivery and Endoscopic Surgeries: Specializing in procedures like arthroscopy and laparoscopy.
  • Physiotherapy Clinic: Comprehensive rehabilitation services.
  • In-House Ambulance Service: Immediate and efficient emergency response.

Our Specializations

Initially focused on core orthopedic and gynecological services, P&P has expanded to include plastic surgery and a comprehensive polyclinic, offering a wide range of medical services under one roof. Our areas of expertise include:

  • Maternity Care: Pregnancy care, family planning, laparoscopic surgery, painless labor, sonography, safe abortion, and cancer surgery.
  • Additional Services: Pregnancy Clinic, Infertility Centre, Daycare Lab, Pharmacy, and Online Consultations.

Achievements

P&P is proud to have been recognized as the Best Hospital by TOI in 2018. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our original research publications, including a notable paper on shoulder treatment.

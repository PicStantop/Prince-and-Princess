WHO WE ARE

Maruti Nursing Home - Best Maternity Hospital in Mulund Welcome to Maruti Nursing Home (MNH)


Proudly rated as “Best-in-Class” among nursing homes in Mumbai. Since our establishment in 2009, we have been dedicated to providing top-notch healthcare services with a personal touch.
Our state-of-the-art facilities include the latest in Digital X-rays (DR), advanced Sonography, and Hi-Tech Operation Rooms. To better accommodate our growing number of outpatients, we have expanded to include new consulting rooms at Maruti NX Polyclinic (MNX), conveniently located close to the hospital. In an era where corporate medical care often lacks personal attention, MNH stands out by delivering compassionate, individualized care. We uphold the trust and value that nursing homes bring to healthcare, ensuring each patient feels cared for and respected.
